Women Hygiene

1. Menstrual Hygiene

Maintaining menstrual hygiene is essential for overall health and well-being. Here are some tips:

  • Change sanitary pads or tampons every 4-6 hours.
  • Wash hands before and after changing menstrual products.
  • Use clean and safe menstrual products.
  • Maintain personal hygiene by bathing regularly.

2. Personal Hygiene

Good hygiene habits help prevent infections and promote health.

  • Shower daily and keep intimate areas clean.
  • Wear breathable cotton underwear to reduce infections.
  • Avoid using harsh soaps or douches.
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.

3. Healthy Practices

Following a healthy lifestyle contributes to better hygiene and well-being.

  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Exercise regularly to improve circulation and overall health.
  • Practice safe hygiene habits to prevent infections.
